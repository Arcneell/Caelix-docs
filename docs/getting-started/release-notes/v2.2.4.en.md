# Release notes — v2.2.4

**Date**: July 2026
**Image**: `ghcr.io/arcneell/caelix:2.2.4` (also `:latest`)

> **HA failover** robustness: the floating VIP moves much faster when a node crashes
> or is stopped. No configuration change required.

---

## Faster VIP failover

Measured live on the 3-node cluster (VIP unavailability window):

| Scenario | Before | After |
|----------|--------|-------|
| **Node crash** (hard loss) | ~39 s | **~5–18 s** |
| **Graceful agent stop** (maintenance) | ~20 s | **~9 s** |

No VIP double-bind observed across the failovers.

### What changed

- **VIP rebind on the fast cadence**: the agent reconciles the VIP every ~5 s
  (heartbeat loop) instead of waiting for the full reconcile pass (15 s), so a new
  leader binds the VIP almost immediately after election.
- **VIP is no longer released during the election gap**: when the leader key is
  briefly empty (old leader gone, successor not yet elected), the current holder keeps
  the VIP instead of dropping it — no more flapping on every failover.
- **Short, frequently-renewed leadership lease**: the lease drops to 15 s (renewed
  ~every 5 s), so after a crash the leader key expires twice as fast and a survivor
  takes over sooner — with no risk of losing leadership on a slow tick, because renewal
  is decoupled from the heavy pass.
- **Immediate resignation on a clean stop**: when a node's agent stops it deletes its
  heartbeat; the controller treats that as a definitive signal and **resigns at once**
  (instead of waiting for the 30 s TTL + hysteresis). A merely *stale* heartbeat
  (transient blip) keeps the anti-flapping hysteresis.
- **Leadership decoupled from rescheduling**: leadership upkeep (resign / acquire) runs
  on the fast cadence; heavy workload placement stays on the slow interval — a failover
  is picked up in ~5 s, not a full interval.
- **Anti split-brain (H1)**: on a failed lease renew, the old lease is **revoked before**
  a new one is minted, so the controller never acts as leader under a fresh session
  while a stale leader key lingers under a dying lease.

## Tunables (safe defaults, rarely changed)

- `CAELIX_LEADER_TTL` (default 15 s): leadership lease TTL.

## Validation

Repeated hard and graceful failovers **on the 3-node cluster**; quorum, encrypted
secrets and etcd auth preserved. Backend + bats suites green (HA tests added: lease
revoke, immediate/hysteresis resignation, VIP held during the gap).
